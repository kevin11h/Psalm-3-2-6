# Psalm-3-2-6
But you, LORD, are a shield around me, my glory, the One who lifts my head high. I call out to the LORD, and he answers me from his holy mountain I lie down and sleep; I wake again, because the LORD sustains me. I lie down and sleep; I wake again, because the LORD sustains me.
